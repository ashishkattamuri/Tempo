## Description (4000 chars max)

Life doesn't follow a schedule. Tempo does.

Tempo is a smart daily planner that automatically adapts your schedule when real life interrupts. Late start? Back-to-back meeting ran long? Unexpected task dropped in your lap? Tempo quietly reshuffles your day so your priorities survive, your habits hold, and nothing important gets lost.

**Your tasks, ranked by what matters**

Tempo organizes everything you need to do into four clear tiers:

- Non-Negotiable — fixed commitments that never move (meetings, classes, appointments)
- Identity Habits — the daily practices that define who you are (workouts, journaling, reading)
- Flexible Tasks — important work that can shift when needed
- Optional Goals — aspirations that flow around everything else

When your day runs over, Tempo knows exactly what to protect and what to rearrange.

**Intelligent conflict resolution**

Add a new task and it overlaps with something already scheduled? Tempo instantly suggests the best resolution — move the new task, shift the existing one, or keep both with a clear view of the overlap. You decide. Tempo executes.

**Habits that survive a bad day**

Most planners let habits disappear when life gets busy. Tempo compresses identity habits to their minimum useful duration rather than dropping them entirely. A 60-minute workout becomes a 15-minute session. Your streak stays alive.

**Calendar import**

Pull your Apple Calendar events directly into Tempo as non-negotiable tasks. Your existing commitments land exactly where they belong, automatically protected from being overwritten.

**Recurring tasks built in**

Set tasks to repeat daily or weekly. Tempo handles the scheduling automatically, resolving conflicts across recurring and one-off items together.

**Timeline view**

See your entire day at a glance on a clean hour-by-hour timeline. Overlapping tasks display side by side. Open gaps show exactly how much time you have to fill. The current time indicator keeps you anchored in the present.

**Private by design**

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no cloud sync, no ads. Your schedule is yours.

---

**Why Tempo?**

Most planners are just lists. Tempo understands that a schedule is a living thing — it bends, compresses, and recovers. It helps you end every day having protected what matters most, even when the day tried to take it all away.
