How it works

Add a task. Tempo handles the rest.

01

Schedule your day

Add tasks, habits, and goals to a clean hourly timeline. Tap any slot to create a task right there. Set tasks as recurring and Tempo generates instances automatically.

02

Life happens

An urgent task drops in. An event runs long. Something demands your time. Tempo detects the conflict the moment it's created.

03

Tempo proposes a resolution

Based on priority, Tempo suggests how to resolve the conflict â€” moving the lower-priority item to the next free slot, up to 7 days out. You approve. Nothing moves without your say.

04

Habits are preserved

Daily habits can compress on packed days â€” showing up for 10 minutes still counts. Weekly habits move to a valid day. Optional goals step aside. Your non-negotiables never budge.
